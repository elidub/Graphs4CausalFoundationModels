=================================================================================
MISE RESULTS (Mean ± Std Error)
=================================================================================

DATASET: normal_noise_constant (STANDARDIZED)
----------------------------------------------
CatBoost Baseline:                    1.6464 ± 0.1025  (10 runs)
DOFM full_graph:                      4.0674 ± 0.2079  (3 runs)
DOFM all_unknown:                     2.4595 ± 0.1307  (3 runs)
PlantEval2 full_graph (full dataset): 3.6653 ± 0.1165  (10 runs)
PlantEval2 all_unknown (full dataset):2.2583 ± 0.1051  (10 runs)

FINDINGS:
- CatBoost performs BEST (1.65) - lowest MISE
- Graph conditioning (full_graph) performs WORSE than no graph (all_unknown)
  * full_graph:  4.07 vs 3.67 for full dataset
  * all_unknown: 2.46 vs 2.26 for full dataset
- This is OPPOSITE to typical PlantEval2 results where graph helps


DATASET: normal_noise_constant_no_std (NO STANDARDIZATION)
-----------------------------------------------------------
CatBoost Baseline:                     129.3038 ± 6.8964  (10 runs)
PlantEval2 full_graph:                 291.3793 ± 5.4624  (10 runs)
PlantEval2 all_unknown:                182.7413 ± 9.8908  (10 runs)

FINDINGS:
- Lack of standardization dramatically increases errors (100x higher)
- CatBoost performs BEST (129.3) even without standardization
- Graph conditioning still HURTS performance (291.4 vs 182.7)
- Relative ordering same as standardized version

=================================================================================
KEY INSIGHTS
=================================================================================

1. GRAPH CONDITIONING HURTS ON PLANTEVAL2:
   - Across ALL tested datasets (normal, four_std, constant noise variants)
   - full_graph consistently worse than all_unknown
   - Suggests: either graph is misleading or model doesn't use it effectively

2. CATBOOST IS STRONGEST BASELINE:
   - Outperforms DOFM on all standardized datasets
   - Even with 100x higher errors (no standardization), still beats DOFM

3. STANDARDIZATION IS CRITICAL:
   - 100x error increase without standardization
   - Affects all methods (CatBoost: 1.65→129, DOFM: 2.26→183)

4. LIMITED DOFM RUNS:
   - Only 3 realizations for dofm_full_graph and dofm_unknown_graph
   - Need more runs to confirm patterns (10 would be better)
   
5. CONSISTENT PATTERN:
   - New datasets behave like existing PlantEval2 datasets
   - Constant noise variant doesn't change fundamental dynamics
